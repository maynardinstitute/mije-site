The Maynard Institute: more than 30 years of driving diversity...

For more than 30 years, the Maynard Institute has been driving diversity and inclusion in the media through newsroom training, collaboration and convenings. We seek to change the complexion of the industry by giving media professionals the tools to become more inclusive storytellers, empowering executives and inspiring entrepreneurs.
